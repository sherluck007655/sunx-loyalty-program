üî¢How to Register Serial Numbers

Serial number registration is how you earn points. Each approved serial number gives you points based on the product type and value.

When to Register Serial Numbers

  • After Installation: Complete the installation first, then register
  • Same Day: Register within 24 hours of installation for best results
  • Customer Present: Get customer details while you're on-site
  • Product Working: Ensure the product is functioning properly

Step-by-Step Registration Process

1 Navigate to Serial Numbers: Click "Serial Numbers" in the left sidebar
2 Click "Add New": Click the "Register Serial Number" button
3 Enter Serial Number: Type the exact serial number from the product label
4 Select Product Type: Choose from dropdown (Inverter, Battery, Solar Panel, etc.)
5 Add Customer Information:
  • Customer Name
  • Installation Address
  • Contact Phone Number
  • Installation Date
6 Upload Photos (Optional): Add photos of the installation for verification
7 Review and Submit: Double-check all information and click "Submit"

What Happens After Submission

Timeline Status What's Happening
Immediate üìù Submitted Your submission is received and queued for review
1-3 Days ‚è≥ Under Review Admin verifies serial number and installation details
3-5 Days ‚úÖ Approved Points are added to your account
If Issues ‚ùå Rejected You'll receive feedback on what needs to be corrected

Product Type Points Range Based On

üí∞Payment Process & Timeline

How the Payment System Works

Understanding when and how to request payments, and what happens during the admin approval process.

Payment Basics

Aspect Details Important Notes
Point Value 1 point = $0.10 USD 1000 points = $100
Minimum Request 1000 points ($100) Cannot request less than this amount
Payment Method Direct bank transfer Uses bank details from your profile
Processing Time 5-10 business days Excludes weekends and holidays

When to Request Payment

  • You Have 1000+ Points: Check your dashboard for current balance
  • Profile is Complete: Bank details must be verified
  • No Pending Issues: All serial numbers should be approved
  • Monthly Timing: Best to request at month-end for faster processing

Step-by-Step Payment Request

1 Check Eligibility: Verify you have 1000+ points on dashboard
2 Navigate to Payments: Click "Payments" in the left sidebar
3 Review Payment History: See previous payments and current balance
4 Click "Request Payment": Start the payment request process
5 Enter Amount: Choose how many points to convert (minimum 1000)
6 Confirm Details: Review your bank information and payment amount
7 Submit Request: Click "Submit Payment Request"

What Happens After You Submit

Admin Review Process

1 Day 1: Admin receives your payment request notification
2 Days 1-3: Admin verifies your account and point balance
3 Days 3-5: Payment is processed through banking system
4 Days 5-10: Money appears in your bank account
üí° Best Practice: Request payments monthly rather than immediately when you reach 1000 points. This reduces processing overhead and ensures faster payments.
‚ö†Ô∏è Common Issues: Most payment delays are caused by incorrect bank information. Always double-check your account and routing numbers.
