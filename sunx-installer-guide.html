🔢Serial Number Management

Serial number registration is the core of the loyalty program. Each valid serial number earns you points that can be converted to cash rewards.

How to Register Serial Numbers

Method 1: Manual Entry

1 Navigate to Serial Numbers: Click "Serial Numbers" in the sidebar
2 Click "Add New": Click the "Add Serial Number" button
3 Enter Details: Fill in the serial number and select product type
4 Add Installation Info: Enter customer details and installation date
5 Submit: Click "Submit" to register the serial number

Method 2: QR Code Scanning (Mobile)

1 Open Mobile App: Use the SunX mobile app
2 Tap Scan: Tap the camera/scan icon
3 Scan QR Code: Point camera at the product QR code
4 Verify Details: Check auto-filled information
5 Submit: Tap "Submit" to register

Serial Number Status

Status Description Action Required
✅ Approved Serial number verified and points awarded None
⏳ Pending Under review by admin Wait for approval
❌ Rejected Invalid or duplicate serial number Check details and resubmit
🔄 Resubmitted Corrected and resubmitted for review Wait for re-approval

Points System

  • Inverters: 100-500 points (based on capacity)
  • Batteries: 50-300 points (based on capacity)
  • Solar Panels: 20-100 points (based on wattage)
  • Accessories: 10-50 points (based on type)
💡 Pro Tip: Take clear photos of serial number labels and keep them as backup. This helps if you need to verify details later.

💰Payment & Rewards

Convert your earned points into cash rewards through our secure payment system.

Understanding the Payment System

  • Point Value: 1 point = $0.10 USD (may vary by region)
  • Minimum Payout: 1000 points ($100)
  • Payment Methods: Bank transfer, PayPal, or check
  • Processing Time: 5-10 business days

How to Request Payment

1 Check Balance: Ensure you have minimum 1000 points
2 Go to Payments: Click "Payments" in the sidebar
3 Request Payment: Click "Request Payment" button
4 Enter Amount: Specify how many points to convert
5 Choose Method: Select your preferred payment method
6 Submit Request: Click "Submit" to process

Payment Status Tracking

Status Description Timeline
📝 Requested Payment request submitted Immediate
⏳ Processing Under review and processing 1-3 days
✅ Approved Payment approved and sent 5-10 days
💸 Completed Payment received in your account Complete
💳 Payment Methods: Make sure your bank details or PayPal information is accurate in your profile to avoid payment delays.

🔧Troubleshooting

Common issues and solutions to help you get the most out of the SunX Loyalty Program.

Login Issues

❌ "Invalid credentials" error

  • Double-check your email and password
  • Use "Forgot Password" to reset if needed
  • Ensure Caps Lock is off
  • Clear browser cache and cookies

❌ "Account not verified" message

  • Check your email for verification link
  • Check spam/junk folder
  • Request new verification email
  • Contact support if email not received

Serial Number Issues

❌ "Serial number already registered"

  • Check if you've already submitted this serial
  • Verify the serial number is correct
  • Contact support if you believe this is an error

❌ "Invalid serial number format"

  • Ensure all characters are entered correctly
  • Check for O vs 0 (letter O vs number zero)
  • Remove any spaces or special characters
  • Try scanning QR code instead of manual entry

Payment Issues

❌ Payment request rejected

  • Verify you have minimum 1000 points
  • Check that bank details are complete and accurate
  • Ensure profile information is up to date
  • Contact support for specific rejection reasons

❌ Payment delayed

  • Normal processing time is 5-10 business days
  • Check payment status in your account
  • Verify bank details are correct
  • Contact support if payment is overdue

Mobile App Issues

❌ QR scanner not working

  • Ensure camera permission is granted
  • Clean camera lens
  • Ensure good lighting conditions
  • Hold phone steady and at proper distance
  • Try manual entry if scanning fails

❌ App crashes or freezes

  • Close and restart the app
  • Restart your device
  • Update to latest app version
  • Clear app cache (Android)
  • Reinstall app if problems persist
